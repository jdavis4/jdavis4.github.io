About

Hey! It's Jasmine. I am currently a senior at Wellesley College pursuing a B.A. in Media Arts and Sciences (interdisciplinary Computer Science, Media Studies, and Studio Art degree) with a concentration in Computer Science and a minor in Economics. I'm a developer interested in data visualization, user experience and human-computer interaction. Social causes I am passionate about include animal welfare and outreach to minorities and children within the tech world, exposing them to the wealth of opportunities that are available. Outside of academics, I love fencing, equestrian, and reading. You can often find me out and about in Boston, on the hunt for the best coffee shops.
